About

My name is Denno Louis and I am a passionate software engineer with a knack for crafting immersive experiences through video game development. I have over four years of experience in Unity and familiarity in Unreal Engine. I graduated from the University of Rhode Island with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and honed strong object-oriented programming skills that form the backbone of my game development endeavors.

Proficient in a multitude of programming languages including C++, Java, Python, C#, and web technologies such as Angular and Spring Boot, I am a versatile developer capable of tackling various aspects of game development.

Beyond technical prowess, I possess a deep understanding of data structures and abstractions, enabling me to optimize game performance and create efficient, scalable systems. With a solid grasp of version control using Git, I maintain a streamlined development process, allowing for seamless collaboration and iteration.

Driven by a passion for innovation and a thirst for knowledge, I aspire to continue learning and pushing the boundaries of game development. Whether it's building captivating narratives, designing intricate gameplay mechanics, or optimizing performance for various platforms, I am committed to creating unforgettable gaming experiences that captivate audiences worldwide.
